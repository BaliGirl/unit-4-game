CRYSTAL COLLECTOR GAME

Instructions:


The player will be given a random number at the start of the game. To win, the player must match the random number that was given without going over.


To collect points, click on any of the following four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. Each crystal is worth a value of numbers between 1 and 12.


When the player clicks on a crystal it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.


If the player successfully matches their total score to the random number they were given, they get 1 win. If the player's total score is above the random number the player get 1 loss. Either way the game restarts.
